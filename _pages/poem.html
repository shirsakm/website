In the quiet moments between us, I find a universe of emotions that words can't quite capture,
Every touch from you is a promise, every kiss a delicious whisper of what's to come.
The way your eyes lock with mine makes time stand still, and in those stolen moments,
I lose myself entirely in the warmth of your embrace.
You are my secret indulgence, my forbidden passion, my muse, my heartbeat,
my smile...my everything and I loveee youuu saur saur much my nom nom babyyy~
